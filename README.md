--------------------BitcoinTraderAnaylsis-----------------------------
*Key Findings-
1.Fear periods generated the highest total profits.
2.Extreme Greed had the highest average profit per trade.
3.Buy trades dominated during Fear.
4.Sell trades dominated during Extreme Greed.
5.Sentiment score significantly influenced profitability (validated via t-test/ANOVA).
6.Top traders showed different leverage and position-size behavior than average traders.
7.The ML model successfully identified features associated with winning trades.
*Trading Strategy-
1.Long bias during Fear.
2.Profit-taking or short bias during Extreme Greed.
3.Avoid excessive leverage during Greed.
4.Use sentiment as a predictive feature in algorithmic trading systems.
